Data Analyst


What is a Data Analyst?

The data analyst acts as the organization's gatekeeper, ensuring that stakeholders can comprehend the data and apply it to inform strategic business choices.

What do you need to be a Data Analyst?

Technical abilities include proficiency with spreadsheet programs like Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets, database languages like SQL, R, or Python, and data visualization tools like Tableau or Qlik. To assist collect, measure, organize, and evaluate data, mathematical and statistical knowledge is also helpful.

What are this job's main responsibilites?

Designing and maintaining databases, which involves addressing code issues and other data-related issues. Obtaining data from both primary and secondary sources, then reformatting it such that it can be read by both humans and computers. composing senior leadership reports that clearly convey trends, patterns, and forecasts utilizing pertinent data.
