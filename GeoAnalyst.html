    • The GIS Innovation Analyst position is the lead role for analyzing, maintaining, designing, developing, and implementing GIS process, tools, and applications within the GIS teams to ensure efficiency and quality of GIS data for our customers. This position is primarily responsible for the support, training, and interaction with the internal GIS team to improve daily GIS processing and user development in the understanding and use of Internal GIS tools and process for delivering high quality public safety GIS to our customers. Additionally, the GIS Innovation Analyst will be the primary liaison between the internal GIS user team and our Product, Sales, and Marketing teams. The GIS Innovation Analyst will lead the development and prototyping of GIS tools for daily use within our GIS team. This role is responsible for determining the cadence for tool updates and will manage a process for vetting internal and external suggestions for new tools and processes including enhancements to existing tools working with the product team.
