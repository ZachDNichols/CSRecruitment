GIS Specialist II, for Saint Cloud MN

If your interested in GIS or NG-911 opperations then GeoComm is Minnesota's trail blazer. They've been around since 1995 working with emergency services to protect our communities.

Location:

Job Summary:

This possition is:
  • Full Time
  • Remote

    • The GIS Specialist II is primarily responsible for developing, updating, analyzing, and managing GIS data for public safety use. The GIS specialist works on a variety of GIS analysis, GIS data development, GIS data maintenance and data preparation for use in public safety GIS systems and regularly communicates project status to our customers, so customer service is a key component of the GIS Specialist position. GIS Specialists work closely within functional teams to deliver GIS data and GIS services to our public safety clients across the United States with a strong emphasis on teamwork, customer commitment, sense of urgency, and continuous improvement. Travel as required, less than 15% of hours for the position on an annual average

  • Bachelor’s Degree in GIS ( or four-year degree with GIS minor)
  • 2+ years GIS public safety (or general GIS experience )
  • Proficiency using ESRI software.
  • Valid driver’s license.
